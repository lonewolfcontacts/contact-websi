How it works

  1. Click “Create Your Contact Page”
  2. Fill in your details (name, phone, WhatsApp, email, socials, address, description)
  3. Copy your unique link and QR code for printing or sharing

Note: This starter is static (GitHub Pages). For user signups and persistent listings, connect to a backend (Firebase, Supabase, or a simple server).
